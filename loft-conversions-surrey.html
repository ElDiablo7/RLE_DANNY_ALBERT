Dormer Loft Conversions

A Dormer conversion is an extension to the existing roof, which projects vertically from a sloping roof, creating additional floor space and headroom. This is the most popular type of conversion in Surrey due to its versatility.

  • ✓ Adds significant internal floor area
  • ✓ Allows for full-height en-suite bathrooms
  • ✓ Suitable for most terraced and semi-detached houses
Enquire about Dormer

Velux (Roof Light) Conversions

Often referred to as 'Sky-light' conversions, these are the least intrusive and most cost-effective. We install high-quality Velux windows into the existing roof line without changing the structure significantly.

  • ✓ Usually doesn't require planning permission
  • ✓ Cost-effective and fast implementation
  • ✓ Floods your new room with natural light
Enquire about Velux

Mansard Loft Conversions

A Mansard conversion transforms the entire roof structure. The roof is replaced with a nearly vertical side and a flat top, maximizing the available space. This is ideal for those looking to create an entire new floor.

  • ✓ Maximum space and volume possible
  • ✓ Often used for master suites and offices
  • ✓ Aesthetic appeal that blends with older properties
Enquire about Mansard
